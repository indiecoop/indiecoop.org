FAQ

What is your business model?

None. We derive value from exposure. We don’t need money because we’re not a business. We’re a collective seeking to be fully transparent about our production.

We aren’t looking to generate artificial scarcity or maximize profits. We want all of our written words, programming code, art assets, and design work to be created for and owned by the commons.

Productive and abundant shared creation and knowlege is our model and our currency.

How can I contribute to a project?

Choose a project you’d like to contribute to. You can simply open a Pull Request on the project’s GitHub repository, or reach out to someone on the Slack chat if you have any specific questions or would just like to introduce yourself.

How can I pitch my project?

Tweet us at @indiecoop on Twitter, or simply open a Pull Request on our indiecoop/indiecoop.org GitHub repository to submit a new project.
